  1. Introduction

    Welcome to Macbease, an online platform operated by Macbease Connections Private Limited ("Macbease," "we," "our," or "us"). This Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions governing the payment of a 5% fee on each ticket sold through the Macbease app and website ("Platform") by clubs and communities ("Organizers") and the consequences of non-compliance with this rule

  2. Definitions
    • Macbease: Refers to the Macbease mobile application and website, collectively.
    • Organizers: Clubs and communities using the Macbease Platform to create and manage events.

  3. Payment Of Fees

    Organizers using the Macbease Platform to sell event tickets are required to pay a non-refundable fee amounting to 5% of the total ticket price for each ticket sold. This fee is intended to support the ongoing development and maintenance of the Macbease Platform

  4. Non-Compliance consequences

    • 4.1. Failure to Pay the Fee: If an Organizer fails to pay the 5% fee for ticket sales as required by this policy, the following actions may be taken:
      • Suspension of Event Posting Privileges: The Organizer's ability to post events on the Macbease Platform may be suspended until the outstanding fees are paid.
      • Cancellation of Ongoing Events: In cases of repeated non-compliance or significant unpaid fees, Macbease may reserve the right to cancel any ongoing events organized by the non-compliant Organizer until all outstanding fees are settled.
    • 4.2. Repeated Non-Compliance: In the event of repeated non-compliance with the 5% fee rule, Macbease may take further actions, which may include:
      • Permanent Event Posting Suspension : Macbease may permanently suspend the non-compliant Organizer's ability to post events on the platform.
      • Termination of Account : Macbease may terminate the Organizer's account on the platform, preventing any future use of Macbease services.

  5. Dispute Resolution
